hyPACK 2013:Target Audience
Application Users on Parallel Processing Platforms
Faculty members who would like to initiate research works on Distributed Shared Memory model or PGAS model with GPGPUs
Intermediate to advanced level users of Multi-Core Core Processors and Systems with GPU Accelerator Devices.
Research Scientists who would like to port their codes on Massievely Parallel Processing platforms using Distributed Shared memory platforms.
Students & faculty members who would like to pursue research career on General Purpose Graphic Processing Units (GPGPUs) computing and GPU computing.
Faculty members and Scientists who work on Energy efficiency of HPC systems and performance of applications in Science and Engineering
Faculty members who would like to teach Multi-Core processors, GPGPUs/ GPU Computing & Distributed Shared Memory Models or PGAS Models with GPGPUs
Faculty members who would like to teach programming on GPGPGUs based on Heterogeneous Programming - OpenCL
HPC researchers, faculty members, and students to exchange ideas related to performance on Multi-Core Processors, GPGPUs /GPU Computing and HPC GPU Clusters
HPC users who would like to understand  Tuning & Performance  on HPC GPU Cluser - health monitoring systems
HPC users who would like to understand  Tuning & Performance  on Hybrid Computing Systems
Researchers who would like work on Application & System Benchmarks on Multi-Core processors and GPGPUs/ GPU Computing.
Final year graduate students who would like to do their projects on Multi-Core processors and GPGPUs/ GPU Computing.
