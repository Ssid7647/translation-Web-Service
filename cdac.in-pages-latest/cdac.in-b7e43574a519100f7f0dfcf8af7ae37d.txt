Limited grant is  available which covers travel, and registration fee with concession for deserving candidates and staff members from Government aided Indian Universities/Colleges and Educational Institutes.
Participants who would like to avail this offer are requested to send their Curriculum Vitae (CV) and soft-copy of an authorization letter from the Head of the Institute or Department of the University to the email-id   abcd2014@nitw.ac.in   before   February 25, 2014 .
Applications will be screened and  ONLY  selected candidates will be intimated before  February 25, 2014  to pay the required registration fee.
Last date  to avail this offer is   February 25, 2014 .
The Early Bird Registration is based on  first- cum- first- serve  basis.
Maximum number of participants allowed is  50.
Interested candidates are requested to   download the registration   form and submit the duly filled registration form for  Early Bird Registration  to the address mentioned below not later than  February 25, 2014  together with a crossed Demand Draft (DD) drawn in favor of Director, NIT Warangal  payable at any nationalized bank in  Warangal  (preferably on SBH, NIT-Branch) for the required fee.
Confirmation of Registration will be intimated over email only after realization of payment in the   NIT Warangal   account.
10%  registration fee is charged for cancellation.
The registration fee does  not  include the accommodation charges.
