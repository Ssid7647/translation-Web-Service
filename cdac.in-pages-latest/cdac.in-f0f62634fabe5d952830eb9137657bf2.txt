OPECG-2009
OPECG-2009 Overview:
The HPCFTE Group, C-DAC, Pune are responsible for OPECG-2009.
This is in collaboration with Department of Mechanical Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology Madras, Chennai.
The objective of  five-day  technology workshop  OPECG-2009  is to extract performance of sequential and parallel programs for large-scale applications on Multi-Core processors and GPUs from application perspective.
The technology workshop is aimed to cover classroom lectures in Morning/Forenoon Session and four hours Hands-on in afternoon session on each day.
The  OPECG-2009  hands-on Session was conducted on Multi-Core Systems and Computing Systems with GPGPUs/ GPU Computing Accelerators.
The Class-room lectures were held in Media Resource Centre, Central Library, Indian Institute of Technology Madras, Chennai.
The Hands-on session was held in Computer Aided Engineering (CAE) Laboratory, Machine Design Block, Department of Mechanical Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology Madras, Chennai.
