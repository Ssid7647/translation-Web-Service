AIIMS
e-Courses in Health & Medicine Sciences
This project aims to develop the e-Learning courses on medical sciences for virtual teaching targeting the medical students of UG and PG level.
Benefits of the Project:
Filling gaps in teaching medical science courses in medical colleges
Standardizing teaching & practice
Standardizing Assessment standards & practice
Establishing templates for eventual countrywide rollout
Goals:
The various courses that would be developed, namely:
Renal system
Respiratory system
Gastrointestinal system
Hepatobiliary system
Musculoskeletal system
Central Nervous System
The project also features:
Development of Feedback system
Establishment of Virtual Classrooms in North East Centers (RIMS, Imphal and NEIGRIHMS, Shillong)
Delivery of Video Lectures & Teaching Modules
