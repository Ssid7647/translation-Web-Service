Framework for Healthcare Analytics
Brief Description
Healthcare Analytics Suite is developed on Big Data framework that uses multiple inputs of healthcare data sets to derive metric based insights.
Such insights should enable healthcare providers (hospitals, doctors) and funding agencies (Govts) to standardize best practices on medication, improve the patients experience and institute preventive and corrective measures in the field of healthcare.
Ensuring focus of effort and monetary resources on key result areas will enhance the patient care with reduction of cost.
Main uses and domain
Healthcare Providers, Insurance Agency & Govt. Entity
Features and Technical Specifications
The Healthcare Analytics suite will give the insights about patient profiling, readmission rate, patients-doctors demands by various departments in the hospital.
