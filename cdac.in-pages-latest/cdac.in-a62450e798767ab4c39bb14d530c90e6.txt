PARAM Yuva II
at National PARAM Supercomputing Facility (NPSF), C-DAC Pune caters to more than 1100 users across various scientific/research/academic institutes in India.
Till date NPSF is the only facility of its kind to offer HPC-As-A-Service and has allocated more that 194 crores CPU hours and has processed 3.7 lakh jobs.
This has culminated into 49 PhDs and 322 publications in Journals and conference proceedings.
