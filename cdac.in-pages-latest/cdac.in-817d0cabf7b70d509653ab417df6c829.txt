Governor unveils supercomputing facility at VSSUT
Odishabytes.com
October 24, 2017
Governor Dr SC Jamir inaugurated supercomputing facility at Veer Surendra Sai University (VSSUT) in Burla through videoconferencing from Raj Bhawan here on Tuesday.
Speaking on the occasion, the Governor said, ”The supercomputing facility will be beneficial for the students, faculty members and research scholars.
It will also help in improving the quality of research for enabling them to gain a competitive edge in their career.”
The creation of supercomputing infrastructure will go a long way in facilitating multidisciplinary engineering research, using high performance computing techniques, enabling technologies, high-end computational research and innovation, he added.
