National Conference on Information Technology, Electronics and Management
Date: 20-21 July, 2017
C-DAC Noida is organizing a 02 day "National Conference on Information Technology, Electronic and Management (NCITEM-2017)".
We are pleased to invite interested members to participate in this conference.
All accepted papers will be published in the proceedings and in IASIR-USA journal.
About the Conference:
The 1st National Conference on Information Technology, Electronics and Management (NCITEM-2017) is organized by Centre for Development of Advanced Computing (CDAC), Noida in association with Institution of Communication Engineers and Information Technologists (ICEIT).
The 1st National Conference on Information Technology, Electronics and Management (NCITEM-2017) is a platform for researchers, engineers, academicians as well as industrial professionals from all over the country to present their research results and development activities in Electronics, Information Technology and Management.
This conference will also provide the delegates to exchange new innovative ideas and to explore opportunities for establishing business or research relations
Target Audience:
Academicians, Faculty Members, Research Scholars and students
Business Professionals, Senior Level Managers, Executives
Practitioners in Business and Non-Profit Organizations
Policy Makers, Entrepreneurs, Enterprising Youth, Industrialists, Consultants etc.
Conference Tracks
Track 1: Electronics
Track 2: Information Technology
Track 3: Management
