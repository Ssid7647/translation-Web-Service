Limited accommodation facility for  five  days stay to attend programme is available at participants cost, subject to availability, on  first-cum-first serve basis  in NIT Warangal (NIT-W) Student Hostel and guesthouse.
The NIT-W Guesthouse   double   occupancy rooms ( as indicated in  Category-2  are provided for academic staff or R &D staff from Govt. aided Indian Universities/Colleges & Educational Institutes  on first-cum-first serve basis only exclusively for participants.
The single occupancy accommodation is not available (NA) for  Category-2  type accommodation facility.
Last date to accept requisition for provision of  abcd-2014  accommodation facility is   February 25, 2014 , subject to abcd-2014 registration is completed.
The confirmation of participant's accommodation will be informed on or before   March 03, 2014.
Local transport facility is provided from   abcd-2014 workshop venue   to respective Orgainsers arranged accommodation place & back.
The   abcd-2014   Registration fee  does not  include accommodation charges and will have to be borne separately by the participant.
