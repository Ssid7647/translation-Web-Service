In the News
The Times of India (Pune)
February 06, 2015
Hemant Darbari, executive director of the centre for Development of Advanced Computing (C-DAC), was recently awarded the Vasvik award for his efforts towards the development of computer assisted translation systems, natural language based information extraction system, among others.
The award was presented to Dr Darbari for the year 2012.
Darbari said, "The award is also a reaffirmation of our faith in indigenous technology development and gives succour to our capabilities in multilingual computing as the primary catalyst for the proliferation of iCT in India."
Darbari has successfully implemented projects like machine assisted translation tool (MANTRA), learn Indian languages through artificial intelligence (LILA), Shrutlekhan, a Hindi speech-to-textsystem among others.
