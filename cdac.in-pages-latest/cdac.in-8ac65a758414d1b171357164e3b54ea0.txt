AayurVidnyaana
A knowledge base encompassing all the aspects of Ayurveda.
Structure:
Articles, Research Papers of the stalwarts from all over India
Collection of information related to different aspects of Ayurveda
Digitalized Brihattrayee (Charaka Samhita, Sushrut Samhita, Ashtang Hridaya) in Devanaagaree Script
Video Clips of Therapeutic Procedures like Medicated Emesis, Blood Letting etc.
Photographs of Herbs, Instruments and Diseases
Audio Files of Dhanvantaree Stavana and other Mantra
Features:
Search facility to find out any word from Brihattrayee
Elaborates scientific basis, theory and strength of Ayurveda in a dynamic form
Key topic of different subjects highlighted with their clinical applications
The articles are focused and classified according to the respective subjects to cover maximum subjects of Ayurveda.
E.g. patient assessment, panchakarma, pathogenesis and theory of disease etc.
ShabdaNidhi (Glossary):
A collection of the basic scientific terms used in Ayurveda.
Every term is described with their etymology, implied meaning, contemporary colloquial and clinical application.
