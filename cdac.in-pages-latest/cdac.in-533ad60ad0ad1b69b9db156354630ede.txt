Workshop on Power-Aware High Performance Computing 2017
Date: May 4, 2017 @ C-DAC Knowledge Park, Byappanahalli, Old Madras Road, Bengaluru
Objective:
The key objective of this workshop is to provide a platform for the researchers, academia, and industries to share their thoughts and giving insights in building next generation power-aware HPC systems.
C-DAC, being a champion in high-performance computing wants to take these concerns forward to find the viable elucidation through this workshop under the National Supercomputing Mission (NSM).
Topics include:
Current state-of-the-art and future challenges in power optimization and roadmap.
Power optimization Techniques
Adaptive Power Management perspective in HPC.
Target Audience:
The program is designed for HPC Practitioner, Academics and Researchers.
