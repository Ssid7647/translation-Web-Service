Project Engineer (Tech)
Post:
C-DAC Post Code:
HYD/PE (T)
No. of Posts
01 (One)
Job Location
Hyderabad / New Delhi
Duration of the position
01 (One) year
Educational Qualification & Experience
First class BE / B. Tech/ MCA or equivalent degree in relevant discipline with 2 to 6 years of Post Qualification work experience in relevant areas.
Post Graduate Degree (M.Sc.) or relevant discipline with 3 to 7 years of Post-qualification work experience in relevant areas.
Relevant discipline/domain for the above qualifications:-
Computer Science/ Electronics/ IT/ Computer Application and related domains
Skill Set
Experience in a wide variety of testing efforts, techniques and tools
Demonstrated experience as a tester
Knowledge of testing methodologies and the test life cycle
Ability to carefully follow use cases and technical requirements
Experience with Python/ Java/ C# testing framework processes and tools
Experience with Selenium, JMeter, SOAtest and others
Knowledge of scripting language like PHP, JavaScript, etc
Exposure to eLearning tools and CMS
Must be a disciplined, self-starter and have the ability to execute on assignments independently
Attention to detail with superior written, verbal, and problem solving skills
Excellent organizational and time management skills
Strong capacity for operating in a flexible, dynamic environment where creative and strategic thinking is required
Ability to work in a high pressure environment
Ability to multi-task is essential
Certifications in software testing and related areas would be desirable
Job Profile
Verify the target objectives against pertinent data shared by project teams;
To prepare project guidelines and reports and evaluate system functionalities and potential by generating test cases and carrying out frequent testing
Review and test system load and capacity of existing provisions and frequently monitor the resource utilization
Reviewing specifications, evaluating tools and indent equipments
Carrying out critical review and propose changes in frameworks/ tools
To maintain historical records through reports
Provide suggestions, survey reports etc. for tools/ products/ solutions
To provide technical support for managing components
Maintain repository and record keeping
Project Lead and Module Lead related activities
Any other activity as assigned by FLA/SLA
Annual CTC (Approximate)
Rs.5.6 lakhs up to Rs.7.4 lakhs per annum based on the post qualification relevant experience as per C-DAC norms
Age
Below 37 years as on last date of application submission
