PCAMS-2014 Accommodation
IIT Bombay Hostel Approximate
Tariff Charges (Rs.) for Single Occupancy Per Day#
IIT Bombay Hostel *
Rs. 250/- to Rs.350/-
Limited hostel accommodation is available in IIT Bombay hostels for workshop participants from June 15th to June 21st evening.
This accommodation is available to participants on a first-cum-first-serve basis, at participant's own cost.
The hostel accommodation charges per day per person are approximately Rs. 250 to Rs. 350, payable directly at the arranged hostel on arrival.
Indicate your accommodation requirements (if any) by email to  pcams2014@cdac.in  .
Note that the registration fee does not include accommodation charges, which will have to be borne separately by the participant.
Last date to accept requisition for hostel accommodation is June 06, 2014, subject to registration process for the concerned participant having being completed.
Confirmation of accommodation will be informed on or before June 06, 2014.
No transport facility is provided to reach FTP-2014 accommodation venue from IIT Bombay Hostels.
PCAMS-2014 Contact
Please contact, preferably by electronic mail to  pcams2014@cdac.in  regarding your accommodation requirement.
