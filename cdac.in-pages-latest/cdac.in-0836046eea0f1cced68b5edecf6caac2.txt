Admission Process
C-CAT rank for PG Diploma in Advanced Computing (PG-DAC) September 2021 for Minority community candidates in Bihar under project of BSMFC, Bihar is declared.
About Programme:
Bihar State Minority Financial Corporation Department of Minority Welfare, Government of Bihar and Centre for Development of Advanced Computing (C-DAC) are conducting Post Graduate Diploma in Advanced Computing of duration six months (900 hours) and Certificate Courses in the areas of Advanced Web Technology and Network Security each of four month duration (320 hours) for the candidates belonging to Minority Communities Muslim, Christian, Sikh, Buddhist, Jain and Parsis belonging to state of Bihar.
About C-DAC:
Centre for Development of Advanced Computing (C-DAC) is a Scientific Society of the Department of Electronics and Information Technology (DeitY), Ministry of Communications and Information Technology (MC&IT), Government of India.
Read more
About Advanced Computing Training School:
C-DAC has set up the Advanced Computing Training School (ACTS) Pune to meet the ever-increasing skilled manpower requirements of the IT industry as well as supplement its intellectual resource base for cutting edge research and development.
Benefits:
A total of 100 beneficiaries shall be provided the training under this scheme
A stipend of Rs. 1,500/- per month for minority candidates may be provided to every student during the course duration.
These IT courses are free of cost for the Minority candidates.

Eligibility:
Graduate in Engineering (10+2+4 or 10+3+3 years) in IT / Computer Science / Electronics / Telecommunications / Electrical / Instrumentation, OR
MSc/MS (10+2+3+2 years) in Computer Science, IT, Electronics with Mathematics in 10+2, OR
Graduate in any discipline of Engineering, OR
Post Graduate Degree in Physics / Mathematics / Statistics, OR
MCA, MCM, OR
Post Graduate Degree in Management with graduation in IT / Computer Science/Applications.
The candidates must have secured a minimum of 50% marks in their qualifying examination.
Certificate Courses:
Graduation in Science with Computer Science / Information Technology / Electronics / Mathematics / Physics / Computational Sciences with PCM in XII, or Engineering with Computer Science and Information Technology with minimum 50% in the qualifying degree.
Age of the candidates applying shall be from 18 to 45 years.
Who Should Apply?:
Minority belonging Muslims, Christians, Sikhs, Buddhists, Jain and Zoroastrians (Parsis), notified by Central Government u/s 2 (c) of the National Commission for Minorities Act, 1992 domicile to the State of Bihar.
Total Annual Income of the family must be less than Rs. 4.5 lakhs.
Education qualification as mentioned in Eligibility.
Note:
1) As PG Diploma in Advanced Computing course will be conducted online so required Laptop / PC configuration, hardware, software and network specification to attend the program is given in Section Admission Process Page.
2) Candidates who have already selected for courses under this scheme will not be eligible to apply again.
3) Candidates must belong to Minority Communities of Bihar State and submit domicile certificate of Bihar State at the time of admission.
4) All admission under this scheme are provisional subject to confirmation of Bihar State Minorities Financial Corporation.
