WORKSHOP SPEAKERS :
Workshop speakers are distinguished faculty from IITs, experts from C-DAC, and experts from HPC companies.
TARGET AUDIENCE :
Participants can be faculty of science and engineering departments, post-doctoral fellows, and Ph.D students.
Participants must have a sound knowledge of C, C++ programming.
All participants are required to bring their own laptops to the workshop for hands-on sessions.
