Sports Club Handbook
In view of student welfare activity (SWA) Sports Club has been constituted on 17.03.2016.
This club is responsible for conduction of Sports events in formal / non- formal academic programs at CDAC Noida and they will function under GC (Academics).
The Sports Club will consist of Faculty and Students representatives which are nominated / elected yearly.
The club is organized and administered by student's representatives.
The faculty representative will provide the organizational leadership for the club's activities.
Although the clubs are encouraged to be self -supporting, the CDAC Management provides limited financial support, facility usage as well as guidance.
The GC (Academics) assists the clubs in fulfilling their goals and objectives.
