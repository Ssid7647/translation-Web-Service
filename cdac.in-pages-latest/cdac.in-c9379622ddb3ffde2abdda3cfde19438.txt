Multilingual Computing & Heritage Computing
Localisation
Our in-house research team strives to make the use of Indian languages more efficient.
Our areas of Research are impressive and cover the full gamut of computing: Natural Language Processing tools (such as spell and grammar checkers, lemmatizers, natural query), Search plug-in’s, Semantic Web, Video Technologies, fonts technology, expert writing systems, image processing (Optical Character and Handwritten character Recognition), Speech Processing, Embedded and Mobile Computing to name only a few.
