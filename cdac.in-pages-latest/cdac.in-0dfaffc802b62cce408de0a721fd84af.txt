Motorized Collar Damper for Zonal HVAC Control
Brief Description
An automatic motorized zonal damper has been designed and developed to adjust the conditioned air inflow into a zone in buildings.
The system is automated for maximizing the comfort of occupant (s) in a zone, reduce energy consumption and eliminate the workload of manual damper adjustment of HVAC technician (s)
Main uses and domain
AC Control Solution for Smart Buildings
Features and Technical Specifications
Motor based operation with Wireless (Zigbee) interface
Automatic control of Collar damper through Zigbee
