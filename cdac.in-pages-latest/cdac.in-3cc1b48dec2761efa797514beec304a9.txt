PhotoExaminer (Forensic Multimedia Analysis Tool)
Photo Examiner is a Windows based cyber forensic application for classifying, enhancing, analysing and generating the report of image and video evidences.
Features
Extract metadata of images and videos
Skin tone detection.
Image classification with filters.
Increase Resolution of thumbnails and small images.
Enhance dark images.
Video Source Camera Identification.
Customisable Report.
Generate Hash for Multimedia files.
Supports MOV, MP4, AVI, JPEG, JPG, PNG, BMP and TIFF
Data Carving - Images and Videos.
Basic image enhancements and colour tone correction
Error Level Analysis
Hidden Pixel Identification
Thumbnail Mismatch Identification
Filter for identifying face images
Video frames Extraction
