Goa state IT and Revenue Minister Rohan Khaunte on Thursday said the government is ready to collaborate with Center for Development of Advanced Computing (C-DAC) for installing supercomputers in the colleges of Goa for enhancing high-end research.
PANJIM: Goa state IT and Revenue Minister Rohan Khaunte on Thursday said the government is ready to collaborate with Center for Development of Advanced Computing (C-DAC) for installing supercomputers in the colleges of Goa for enhancing high-end research.
”Today supercomputing has gained lot of importance in research and development.
We are ready to collaborate with CDAC in taking new technologies like supercomputing and virtual reality to the colleges in Goa.
We want our children to learn futuristic technologies as they prepare for newer challenges in the future, ” Khaunte said here.
The Minister was speaking at the inauguration of C-DAC’s latest supercomputer PARAM Shavak-virtual reality, on Thursday.
