Multilingual Computing
Gist
GIST addresses a variety of uses concerned with the use and implementation of Indian languages.
We offer a number of critical components such as Spell checkers, Online thesaurus, ActiveX controls, Java controls and Fonts which have been developed after research and fine tuning over the previous decade.
GIST has been associated in enabling IT equipment with Indian languages and has collaborated to provide Indian language pagers as well as Indian language LCD panels.
Our in-house research team strives to make the use of Indian languages more efficient.
This has led to the development of the Indian language Optical Character Recognition (OCR), Speech to text, Text to speech and other technologies.
Our wide range of products using GIST have been developed to fulfill a particular need.
Our products can be broadly divided into:
Mass Appeal Products
Products for Organizations
Application Development Tools
Video Works
Web Application and Services
Enabling of Handheld Devices/Display Systems
