Forestry and Ecology Eco-informatics
Landuse and Land Cover Dynamics and Impact of Human Dimensions in Indian River Basins
Study Area:  Western Ghats and Krishna River Basin
Project Highlights
Development of Landuse/Landcover (LULC) database for 3 decades (1985-95-05) using multi-temporal satellite datasets (LANDSAT - MSS, IRS LISS 1, and IRS LISS 3) at 1:250000 scale.
Development of LULC change drivers database for three decades (1985-95-05) including Socioeconomic, Climatic and Topographical drivers at 250m grid size
Prediction of future LULC using relationship models
Hydrological Modeling
Comprehensive Spatial Decision Support System (SDSS) for Forest Management-Bodoland Territorial Areas District (BTAD)
Study Area:  Bodoland Territorial Areas District, Assam
Project Highlights
Forest working plan automation
Interactive forest village rehabilitation planning
Carbon pool calculator
Joint forest management
Habitat suitability analysis
Identification of encroachment areas
Forest crime analysis
Comprehensive SDSS- ARANYA
Multicriteria Spatial Modeling for Identification of Potential Afforestation/Reforestation Sites for Claiming 'Carbon Credits' and Analysis of Futuristic Landuse Dynamics
Study Area:  Kamrup & Cachar Districts, Assam
Project Highlights
Identification of potential afforestation/reforestation sites for generation of carbon credits considering eligibility, climatic, ecological and biodiversity criteria
GIS based predictive modeling for future forest cover estimation
Land-Use and Land-Cover Dynamics and Impact of Human Dimension in Western Ghats of India
Study Area:  Western Ghats of India (Gujarat, Daman & Diu, Maharashtra, Goa, Karnataka, Kerala & Tamil Nadu)
Project Highlights
Identification of changes in landuse/landcover during 1985-2005
Detailed investigation of landuse/landcover change drivers
Future landuse/landcover predictions
Study of Landscape Dynamics with Emphasis on Landuse/Landcover Change vis-a-vis Carbon Sequestration and Habitat Loss
Study Area: Western Ghats of Maharashtra
Project Highlights
Detailed investigations of Biomass, Biodiversity and Carbon Pool in a part of Western Ghats of Maharashtra (Radhanagari Wild Life Sanctuary)
Estimation of carbon sequestration potential of different forest types and plantation, located in different strata (based on multi-time observations in permanent sampling locations)
Biodiversity Characterisation at Landscape Level Using Satellite Remote Sensing and GIS
Study Area:  Bihar, Maharashtra (Including Western Ghats), Central India
Project Highlights
Identification of biodiversity hot-spots using principles of landscape ecology and geoinformatics
Preparation of vegetation-type maps and biodiversity characterization
Identifying areas for conservation prioritization based on analysis of biological richness (calculated by taking social, biological, terrain and field datasets into consideration)
Biodiversity Characterisation at Landscape Level using Satellite Remote Sensing and GIS
Study Area:  Proposed Rajmachi (Father Santa Pau) Wildlife Sanctuary, Pune
Project Highlights
Biodiversity characterisation at landscape level
Identification of disturbance regime
Identification of biologically rich areas for conservation prioritization
Study of interrelationship of forests and associated agriculture zones
Identification of potential landraces zones
