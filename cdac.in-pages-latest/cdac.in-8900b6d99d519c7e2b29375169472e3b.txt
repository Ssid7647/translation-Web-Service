Groundwater Prospect Mapping using Remote Sensing Data for Rajiv Gandhi National Drinking Water Mission
Study Area:  Lower Assam
Project Highlights
Groundwater Prospective Mapping at 1:50,000 scale
Maps showing ground water potential zones and tentative locations for constructing recharge structures
Identified groundwater resource for non-covered and partially covered habitations to bring them under drinking water supply scheme (ARDSS-Norm)
Preparation of Generalized Probable Maximum Precipitation (PMP) Atlas over the Krishna River Basin using GIS
Study Area:  Krishna River Basin
Project Highlights
Interpolated PMP values of rain gauge stations to entire basin
Preparation of seasonal & annual rainfall maps, severe rainstorm map, drainage map & isohyetal maps of historical rainstorms
Development of interactive software for estimation of storm parameters & tool for storm transposition on the digital map
