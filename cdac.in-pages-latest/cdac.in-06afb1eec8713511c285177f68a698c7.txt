Biometric Attendance System
Brief Description:
Centralized, web-based Biometric Attendance System (using Iris or Fingerprint for marking the attendance).
Main uses and domain :
Time Attendance for Offices, Colleges, Schools, etc.
Features and Technical Specifications:
Touchscreen-based Bharatiya Biometric Attendance System.
The system also enables identification of persons with spectacles, contact lenses, persons from distance and with wide gaze angles.
Platform required (if any):
Works on Windows and Linux.
