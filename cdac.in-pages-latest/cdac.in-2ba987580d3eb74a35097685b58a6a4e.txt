Specialization/ Domain
C-DAC Adjunct Engineer - IOT Technologies
No. of Requirements
Job Location
Desired Educational Qualification and specialization
M.Tech in Electronics and Communications/ Embedded Systems
Experience
8 - 10 years of relevant experience
Max Age
57 years at the time of the submission of application
Competence (one or more from the list)
Experience in C programming, Embedded C, Hardware design and debugging, Schematic verification, device driver develoment, board support package development
System/module/product development using 32-bit microcontrollers/SoC
Experience in handling various protocols: Serial Protocols – UART, SPI, I2C, IoT Protocols, Wireless Sensor Networks, Long and short range Wireless technologies, Analog and Digital Communication, ZigBee/IEEE 802.15.4, Wi-Fi/IEEE 802.11, Bluetooth protocols
Digital Modulation techniques, Error Detection and Correction schemes, Logic Design, Embedded systems.
VLSI with Verilog HDL, FPGA
Networking tools like NS3, CISCO Packet Tracer,
EDA tools like Zuken CADSTAR
Knowledge of cross platform development tools & IDEs like Keil, IAR embedded workbench etc.
Static and dynamic code analysis,
EMI/EMC testing processes,
Good knowledge product compliance test standards and testing processes
Python programming
Journal and Conference publications of repute.
Expectations
Ideate, conceptualize and create a techno-commercial innovative solutions using IoT technologies.
End to end managment of product prototyping, development lifecycle managment, Version control tools
Excellent oral and written communication skills.
Knowledge and experience on technical publications and patent process.
Consolidated Pay Per Month
As per the industry standards based on qualification, experience, expertise, role etc.
