e-Mahashabdkosh
e-Mahashabdkosh is domain based Bi-Lingual and Bi-Directional Hindi/English dictionaries with pronunciation.
These dictionaries provide prevalent pronunciation and information of Hindi words and phrases.
For each English entry, its equivalent Hindi meaning (one–to–many is provided.
These dictionaries provide much essential information that is not available in a general dictionary, such as the pronunciation, inflected forms, usage and description of a word.
e-Mahashabdkosh can be accessed at http://e-mahashabdkosh.rb-aai.in .
A link is also provided on the Rajbhasha Vibhag websitehttp://rajbhasha.gov.in/
Salient Features:
Pronunciation of searched word
Clear layout/GUI, Easy navigation
Words Listed at 3 character combination
Direct word search
Bidirectional search
Facility to search within the searched list of words
Meanings and relevant information
Usage of the word/phrases
Definition of the word
