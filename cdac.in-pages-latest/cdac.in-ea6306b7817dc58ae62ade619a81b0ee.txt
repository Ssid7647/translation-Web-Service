Content Contribution, Moderation & Approval Policy (CMAP)
Content needs to be contributed by the authorized Respective Designated Responsible Member (DRM) from various centers of C-DAC in a consistent fashion to maintain uniformity and to bring in standardization along with associated metadata and keywords.
The content on the portal goes through the entire life-cycle process, which consists of:-
Creation
Modification
Publishing
Expiry
Archival
Once the content is contributed it needs to be approved and moderated prior to being published on the Website.
The moderation could be multilevel and is role based.
If the content is rejected at any level then it is reverted back to the originator of the content for modification.
Different Content Element is categorized as: -
Routine - activities that are done as a normal part of a job or process.
Priority - activities that are done as a urgent part of a job or process
Express - activities that are done as a most urgent part of a job or process
(Approver and the moderator for each of the content element)
