IOT Labkits
Lab kits
1. IOT Research lab kit
The Systems described above were packaged into a research lab kit for experimentation in the areas of IOT.
The Research kit contains the following modules
Ubimote:10 Nos
Wi-Fi Mote:10 Nos
BLE mote:10 Nos
WINGZ:1 No
Ubi-Sense:10 Nos
Ubi-DAQ:10 Nos
Debugger (Optional)
2. WSN (Wireless Sensor Network) Research lab kit
If the researchers are looking for single communication standard based research, this kit will be useful with the following combination
Ubimote -30 Nos
Ubi-Sense: 10 Nos
3. IOT research lab table set up
Apart from the above two research kit variants a simple research table set up is being provided with a total cost of Rs.50000.00 (Rupees fifty thousand only) comprising of 6 Nos of Ubimote, 3 Nos of Ubi-Sense, 1 No. of Ubi-DAC along with a programming cable.
Possible list of experiments with the above research kits and IOT lab table includes
1.Embedded Programming
1.1.Toggling LEDs
1.2.Transmitting a string through UART
1.3.Controlling LEDs blinking pattern through UART
1.4.Echo each character typed on serial terminal.
1.5.Digital IO configuration.
1.6.Timer based LED Toggle.
1.7.On-chip Temperature measurement through ADC.
2. RF experiments
2.1.Point to point communication of two C-Motes over the radio frequency.
2.2.Multi-point to single point communication of C-Motes over the radio frequency.
3. Experiments on interfacing with UbiSense
3.1.I2C protocol study
3.2.Reading Temperature and Relative Humidity value from the sensor.
3.3.Reading Light intensity value from light sensor.
3.4.Reading of atmospheric pressure value from pressure sensor.
3.5.Proximity detection with IR LED.
3.6.Generation of alarm through Buzzer.
3.7.Transmitting the measured physical value from the UbiSense over the Air.
4. Experiments on interfacing with Ubi-DAC
4.1.Timestamp with RTC
4.2.IO Expander
4.3.Relay control.
4.4.I2C based 12-channel ADC
4.5.EEPROM read and write
5. WSN Applications
5.1.Demonstration of a peer to peer network topology using Coordinator and end device network device types.
5.2.Demonstration of peer to peer communication between Coordinator and end device through Router.
5.3.Establishing Many to one Communication ( Star Network Topology)
5.4.Establishing Tree Network Topology
5.5.Establishing Cluster Tree Network
6. IOT applications
6.1.Porting 6lowPAN stack on Ubimote for enabling it with IPv6
6.2.6lowpAN network formation with motes and PC
6.3.IP based lighting control through DAC
6.4.IP based sensor monitoring through Ubi-Sense
Apart from the above listed, experiments on Bluetooth, Wi-Fi and heterogeneous network setup for IOT and interoperability achievement among various protocols can be tried on IOT research lab kit.
