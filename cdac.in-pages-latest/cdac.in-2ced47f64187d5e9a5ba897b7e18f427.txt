Mobile e-governance Service Delivery Gateway -III
Brief Description:
C-DAC created integrated platform under mobile governance for offering services over mobile based channels to the citizens, the MeitY has, in October 2018, sanctioned MSDG phase III project so that other new services can be added in the platform and offered to citizens of India.
Main uses and domain :
Mobile Governance
Features and Technical Specifications:
