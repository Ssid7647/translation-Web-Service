Cyber Security Division of C-DAC Bangalore under the leadership of Dr. Hemant Darbari, Director General and the expert guidance of Shri GL Ganga Prasad, Senior Director & Centre Head, C-DAC, Bangalore conducted a workshop on IPv6 for Senior Officials of JNPT 1st August 2018 at Jawaharlal Nehru Port Trust (JNPT) Mumbai.
The workshop focused on various topics including IPv6 Addressing, Comparison of Ipv6 and IPv4, IPv6 Transition Technologies, ICMPv6 and IPv6 Error handling, IPv6 Application and Services.
Hands-on sessions were conducted with focus on Stateless and Stateful IPv6 Configuration, Web application on IPv6 and DNS configuration for Ipv6.
Senior officials from various divisions of JNPT attended the training.
Shri. Mrinal K Sarkar CIO JNPT inaugurated the workshop.
Shri. Suhas Kamitkar of MS Division JNPT delivered the introductory talk.
The sessions were delivered by Shri. Gokul Theerthan M, & Shri. Sanjay Adiwal from C-DAC Bangalore.
The event was coordinated by Shri. Dittin Andrews (CNIE) under the guidance of Dr. Kumari Roshni V.S ., Senior Director, C-DAC, Bangalore
