Vigilance Awareness Week
This year Vigilance Week is being observed from 26 Oct to 1 Nov 2021.
To create awareness among the C-DAC employees and their family members following competitions are planned to be organised:
Poster making competition for children of employees - [class 5 to 7]
Collage competition for children of employees - [class 8 to 10]
Slogan writing competition on Topic 'Vigilance approach prevents Corruption' for Spouse of employees.
Online quiz for employees
