Brief Description
Short term open access (STOA) is for the scheduling of bilateral transactions as a part of energy trading.
STOA comprises the entire workflow for processing the power requisition applications and helps in maximizing the power grid utilization.
Following are the major functionalities of the STOA workflow solution
Online filing of applications by applicants
Forwarding concurrence request to the concerned Load Dispatch Centres (LDC) and receiving the concurrence
Processing of applications for approval, pro-rata calculations based on availability
Generation of payment schedule
Provision of generating of energy schedule for next day
Maintaining the curtailment details in case of transmission constraints
Role based user administration
Main uses and domain
Power Sector - Energy Trading
Features and Technical Specifications
Web based software
Platform required (if any):
Server with Application Server, MySql database
