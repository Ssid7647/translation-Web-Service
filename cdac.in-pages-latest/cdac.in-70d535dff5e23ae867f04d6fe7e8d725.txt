Brief Description
HF transmitter is a HF power amplifier unit with all desirable features in addition to monitoring and protection circuits.
Main uses and domain
This product is intended to be used by Defence and other professional domains
Features and Technical Specifications
Features:
Freq. range: 3MHz-30MHz
6 Bank harmonic filter
Transmit power 100W
Tunable receive front end with co-site filters
Transmit power : 100W
Harmonics :< 60dBc
Power control :+/-1dB
