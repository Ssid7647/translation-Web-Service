About VIT:
VIT Chennai is a globally engaged, competitive, comprehensive, research-enriched university campus strategically positioned to respond to major industrial, social, economic and environmental demands and challenges.
Courses:
Following Courses will be offered:
Masters Programmes:
M.Tech in High Performance Computing
M.Tech in AI & Machine Learning
M.Tech in Cyber Physical Systems
Certificate Programmes:
Certificate Programmes will be conducted as integrated programmes for second year and third year students of B.Tech CSE, ECE and IT branch.
Following courses will be offered:
Certificate Course in BigData Analytics (CCBDA)
Certificate Course in Cyber Forensics & Cyber Laws (CCCFCL)
Certificate Course in Ethical Hacking and Information Security (CCEHIS)
Campus:
The center is located at the picturesque location between the VIT, Chennai campus.
The center is well equipped with world class state-of-the-art facilities.
Spacious full-fledged labs are functioning under the guidelines of Experts.
