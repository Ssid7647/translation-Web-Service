C-DAC: GIST - Services - Credit Titling
The Video Service Cell provides services in the field of Credit Titling in various Indian languages by using our well-appreciated Multilingual titling system MOVE CG 2001.
Supports English and up to 10 Indian scripts
16 million colors for foreground and background colors with 256 levels of transparency
Bold, italics, underline, highlight and reverse video attributes & four basic character sizes
Scrolling and crawling at nine different speeds and variety of special effects
Allows to input background image on which the credit title can be overlaid
Component, Composite and S-VHS video I/O interface
