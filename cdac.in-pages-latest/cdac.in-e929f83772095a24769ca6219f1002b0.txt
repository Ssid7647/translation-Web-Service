Name of Post
CAE (8 years and above)
Specialization/ Domain
C-DAC Adjunct Engineer - Cyber Security Specialist
No. of Requirements
Job Location
Desired Educational Qualification and specialization
BE/ B. Tech / MCA in IT/Computer Science / Electronics / E&TC
Post Qualification relevant Experience
8 years & above
Max Age
57 years at the time of the submission of application
Desired Skill Sets
Essential
In-depth technical understanding and hands-on experience in analysing logs, network data (EG.
Netflow) and other attack artifacts in support of incident investigations.
Experience with vulnerability scanning, assessment solutions.
Understanding and implementation knowledge of ISMS policy (Information System Management System)
Investigations of Security breach alert.
Good verbal and written (English) communication skills.
Shall be aware of various Cyber Security Implementation to protect IT infrastructure
Defining the security framework for the organization
Drafting of various IT Security Policies and processes.
Desirable
Candidate should have knowledge of the following
Advanced understanding of TCP/IP, common networking ports and protocols, traffic flow, OSI model, defense-in-depth and common security elements.
Understanding of DLP, HIPS, IDS/1PS, Full Packet Capture, Host & Network based Forensics and RSA Security.
Knowledge of SOC solutions.
Knowledge of various Security Standards,
Understanding of various Data encryption algorithms.
Proposed Job Profile
Reporting the security analysis and findings.
Maintaining the regulatory systems about ISMS policy (Information System Management Systems)
Maintaining and implementation of SOC for Network Security.
Status reports as per designated policies.
Recommend modifications in legal, technical and regulatory areas that affect IT security.
Monitoring of web security gateways, perimeter security, network access controls, endpoint security, SOC Analysis reports etc.
Coordinating with CISO and other Networking Teams, Understanding of Secure DNS and Network Monitoring and Management Tools.
Knowledge of one or more of following would be beneficial.
Linux Server systems
Basic scripting skills to customize Monitoring and integrating of S/W
Certifications (One or more of following or equivalent Certifications)
Advanced certifications such as SANS GIAC/GCIA/GCIH, CISSP or CASP
Hold DoD-85870 IAT Level 2 baseline certifications (Security+CE or equivalent)
Certified Network Defender (CND)
Certified Ethical Hacker (CEF)
Certified Chief Information Security Officer (CISO)
Certified Security Specialist/Analyst
Certified Network Defense Architect (CNDA)
Consolidated Pay Per Month
As per the industry standards based on qualification, experience, expertise, role etc.
