C-DAC’s Toolkit for LOINC is a specially designed FOSS toolkit for easy access and integration of LOINC standard in health care applications.
Logical Observation Identifiers Names and Codes (LOINC®) is an international standard for identifying health measurements, observations, and documents.
The toolkit provides simple to use APIs for LOINC integration.
It enables clinicians and researchers to find out relevant LOINC codes from its components including, long common name, short name, display name, and other related information.
Architecture
The toolkit is designed to be easily programmable allowing developers to build sophisticated and complex applications on top of it.
The package includes the deployable, supporting documents, and integration examples.
