Three Projects of CDAC Kolkata have received the eAssam Challenge 2015 Digital Innovation for Development Certificate of Presentation at the 1st eAssam Summit held at NEDFI House, Dispur, Guwahati on 10th April 2015.
All the three projects have been reviewed and screened by the screening committee for participation and presentation at the eAssam Summit.
All the three projects have been presented by Shri Goutam Kumar Saha, Scientist-E & Chief Investigator of the following three projects.
Project-1:  Deployment of Health Care Knowledge System in the North-East States for Promoting Public Health Awareness & Education, Category: Digital Innovation in Health & Environment.
Team Members:  Shri Goutam Kumar Saha (ScE & CI), Shri Sandeep Kumar, Shri Manish Kumar Saha, Shri Pranab Ranjan Chakraborty (Software Technology Group).
eAssam 2015 three Certificates Received by Shri Goutam Kumar Saha, GH-STG
Project-2: IT based Computer Aided Design (CAD) Centre for Creative Design & Development by Artisans/ Weavers of Tripura, Assam & Arunachal Pradesh Category: ICT for Livelihood, Skill & Enterprise.
Team Members:  Shri Goutam Kumar Saha (ScE & CI), Shri Raja Gupta (Co-CI), Shri Pranab Ranjan Chakraborty, Shri Krishna Das, Shri Sandeep Sen, Shri Indrajit Karmakar, Shri Asit Singh, Shri Sabyasachi Majumder, Shri Angshuman Chakraborty, Shri Koustav Chatterjee (Software Technology Group).
Project-3: Centralized e-Auction System for North Eastern States (Sikkim, Assam, Arunachal Pradesh, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Manipur, Tripura and Nagaland) Category: E-Governance & Citizen Services.
Team Members: Shri Goutam Kumar Saha (ScE & CI), Shri Debdulal Basak (Co-CI), Shri Manas Chakraborty, Shri Raja Gupta, Shri Sandeep Kumar, Shri Manish Saha, Shri Krishna Das, Shri Sandeep Sen, Shri Niladri Sekhar Saha, Shri Samaresh Das, Smt. Shimantika Shah, Shri Sandeep Sen, (Software Technology Group, C-DAC Kolkata).
