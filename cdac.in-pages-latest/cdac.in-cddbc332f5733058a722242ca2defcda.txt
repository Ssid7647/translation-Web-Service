CTSF - Remote Usage Facility
Users can opt for one or more of the following options to access the CTSF resources remotely:
Establishing a 56.6 Kbps dialup link over PSTN (Public Switched Telephone Network).
Establishing a dedicated 128 Kbps link over ISDN (Integrated Services Digital Network).
Establishing a 64 Kbps leased line terrestrial circuit between remote locations and C-DAC through the Fixed Telecom infrastructure.
Providing a secure login via the Internet.
