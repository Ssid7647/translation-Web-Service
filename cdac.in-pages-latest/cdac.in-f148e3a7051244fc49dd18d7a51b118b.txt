INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CONSULTANCY CLINIC ERP Programme
Education and Training
Education Technologies
Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) Programme
Objective
ERP Programme at CDAC Noida is aimed to upgrade skills of working professionals in the area of Human Resource, Material Management, Production Planning, Financial Accounting, Sales and Distribution, Information Technology.
The objectives of the programme are:
Impart intensive training on ERP
Upgrade skills of working professionals in handling Business Processes
Develop skills in Client Server Technology
Apart from these areas, we also provide training in the areas such as Customer Relationship Management, Supply Chain Management, Management Accounting and ERP Solution Management.
Highlights
Full time Program
Licensed Software
Dedicated System for Individuals
Dedicated Faculty For each Module
Course Material Provided
Hostel & Canteen Facility Available
Placement Assistance
Duration & Timing
The duration for each module is 5 weeks full time.
The classes are held five days a week from 9:30 AM to 5:30 PM (Mon - Fri)
Fee Structure
Fee for each module is Rs.40, 000/- + service tax 14%.
Payable in two equal installments.
Eligibility
Graduates Engineers, MBA's/MCA's or Chartered/ Cost Accountant with one year Experience with Knowledge of computer applications can apply.
Note:- Freshers can apply for Software Development and Business Intelligence module
Selection & Registration for Program
Selection will be based on personal interview / counseling to be conducted at CDAC Noida, Once selected, one can register in advance on First-Come-First-Serve basis by submitting 50% of the fee as Registration Fee either in Cash or by Demand Draft in favor of CDAC, Noida
Documents Required
Duly filled in Registration form.
Copy of Professional Qualification
Copy of Experience Certificate (if Any)
Character Certificate
2 stamp size photographs.
Once registered, any request for cancellation must be made at least 2 weeks before the start date of the batch.
In this case a Processing fees of Rs 1000/- will be deducted from the Registration fees.
There is no refund if there is no cancellation request before 2 weeks of the start of the batch.
CERTIFICATE OF PARTICIPATION
On successful completion of the programme, all participants are awarded a participation certificate from CDAC.
