Electronics Standards of India for DeitY
The online application will be submitted through ESI System by an Manufacture for registration, renewal, surveillance & testing, Lab Registration etc.
DeitY has issued first order under Compulsory Registration Scheme of Department of Consumer Affairs (DOCA)/BIS mandating compliance to Indian safety standards for 15 electronic items.
In the DeitY Electronics & Information Technology Goods (Requirements for Compulsory Registration) Order, 2012 itself under clause 11, a commitment has been made to handle the whole process of Registration, Surveillance, etc. in a transparent and time bound fashion.
This System enables online submission and scrutiny of applications submitted to the Deity under the ESI of schemes for 15 electronic items.
ESI is a web-based system which supports processes for:
Online submission of Manufacture registration, renewal, surveillance & testing, Lab Registration etc. Evaluation of proposals
Evaluation of by BIS officials
Project Management
