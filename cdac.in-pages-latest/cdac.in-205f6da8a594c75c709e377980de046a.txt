Anvaya
A Workflow Environment for High Throughput Comparative Genomics: Anvaya is a software application consisting of interface to Bioinformatics tools and databases in a workflow environment, to execute the set of analyses tools in series or in parallel.
Big Data in Biology
Biological Bigdata management and analytics become essential to produce a wealth of fruitful information beneficial for healthcare

Digital Biology
An approach to address the large scale of data in the field of Biology

GIST Linguistic Translator Workbench
Linguistic Translator workbench is a powerful and easy to use translation editor equipped with built in translation assets like Prediction engine, Named Entity Identification and Tagging, Transliteration, Translation Memories, Glossary, WordNet to name a few.
GoTranslate

Kanthasth
"Kanthasth" is a module which provides a framework which stores bilingual data (English-Hindi) from all over India, on a centralised repository.
The Translation Memory, ensures the reusability of the existing data, in-turn, providing increased accuracy & efficiency of translation.
LILA
Learn Indian Languages through Artificial intelligence is multi-media based intelligent self-tutoring application for learning Hindi.
LILA Pravah
LILA Hindi Pravah is a Web & Mobile based self-tutoring system which includes a collection of narration of few prominent stories and poems of renowned writes of India.
Meri Sadak App
A citizen feedback system under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana.
PARAM Shavak VR
promises to take the entire experience of research and learning to a new level through interactive VR experiences like simulation and provide greater scientific insights.
PARAM SHIVAY
PARAM SHIVAY is a supercomputing system developed under NSM build approach is inaugurated by Hon'ble Prime Minister Shri. Narendra Modi at IIT (BHU), Varanasi.
PARAM Shreshta
PARAM Shreshta is an entry level supercomputing system designed by C-DAC under National Supercomputing Mission keeping an eye onm the computational needs of the various research, engineering and MSME industries with a peak compute power of 100 TeraFlops.
PARAM Yuva II
at National PARAM Supercomputing Facility (NPSF), C-DAC Pune caters to more than 1100 users across various scientific/research/academic institutes in India.
Till date NPSF is the only facility of its kind to offer HPC-As-A-Service and has allocated more that 194 crores CPU hours and has processed 3.7 lakh jobs.
This has culminated into 49 PhDs and 322 publications in Journals and conference proceedings.
