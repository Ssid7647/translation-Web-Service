PCAMS-2014 Registration (Reg.
for Five Days is open)
Registration form, DD, along with authorization certificate should reach The Workshop Coordinator by 6th June 2014
The  PCAMS-2014   registration fee includes soft-copy of proceedings, lunch, and refreshments.
5 Days
(June 16-20, 2014)
Fees
Per Participant*
INR 7,500
The Registration fee per participant is Rs. 7500.
The registration fee includes softcopy of course contents, lunches and refreshments on all five days of the workshop.
It does not include any accommodation charges.
The registration fee is non-refundable.
To register, download registration form, and fill it.
Obtain an authorization letter from the Head of your university/institute.
Make a Demand Draft (DD) drawn in favour of Registrar, IIT Bombay payable at any nationalized bank in Mumbai (preferably on SBI, IIT Powai Branch, Mumbai) for the registration fee amount.
Send all these materials by courier to the workshop coordinator at IIT Bombay (address given below).
