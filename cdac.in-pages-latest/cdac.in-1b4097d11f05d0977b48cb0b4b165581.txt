India – Egypt Centre of Excellence in IT
Project funded by MEA with CDAC as Indian Implementing Agency & Al Azhar University in Cairo as Egyptian Implementing agency.
Agreement between DPA-II Division, MEA & CDAC signed on March 30, 2016.
Project consists of:
IT infrastructure and supply of course material.
Deputation of CDAC experts at CEIT for Centre Coordination & Course Delivery
Workshops at CEIT in Egypt on e-Governance, e-Commerce, e-Learning, Ubiquitous Computing, Cloud Computing & Bio-Medical
