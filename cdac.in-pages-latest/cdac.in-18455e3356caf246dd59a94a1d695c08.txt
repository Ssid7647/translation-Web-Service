e-Content Generation & e-skill Test
e-Content Generation & e-skill Test in Specialized Areas of Information Technology
Design & Development of e-learning content in specialized areas of Information Technology to be used through Sakshat portal of MHRD to benefit learners from the e-Learning facility of CDAC Noida for accessing the contents anytime suiting their requirements.
This is a sponsored project under NMEICT, MHRD, for generating awareness & skilled man power in the below mentioned Information Technology areas.
Deliverables of the Project
E-Learning content has been developed in the following areas of Information Technology:
6 Courses of GIS and Remote Sensing
7 Courses of Advanced Software Design and Development
6 courses of System and Database Administration
7 courses of Information Security and Application Development
E-Learning content of each course consists of 40 hours duration each, with textual explanations, visual images, video lectures, animations, audio explanations and activity based self assessment tests.
Course Access Method
The courses can be freely accessible at: "http://elearn-mhrd.cdacnoida.in".
Click the Learner's Corner tab.
After logging in it will display the courses list.
Then select View Content option from a particular course.
And then click the e-Content or Video Lecture link provided on the course page.
Features of e-Content
The e-Learning content consists of the following features: Based on the Four Quadrant Model of e-Learning provided by MHRD, Every page has Textual Explanation, and explanation in the form of Video Lectures and Voice over Explanation, Each course has Self Assessment Tests & Quizzes, Simulative Explanations and the content has full compliance to SCORM 2004 e-Learning standard.
The e-Content is free of Cost and available 24x7.
