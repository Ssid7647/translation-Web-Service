CAE-1
Specialization/ Domain
C-DAC Adjunct Engineer - VLSI & Embedded System
No. of Requirements
Job Location
Desired Educational Qualification and specialization
M. Tech. in VLSI Systems / Ph.D. in Microelectronics/VLSI Systems
Experience
8 - 10 years of relevant experience
Max Age
57 years at the time of the submission of application
Competence (one or more from the list)
Verilog HDL coding and Verification
C, Matlab coding and simulations
EDA tools Mentor Graphics/Cadence/Synopsys
FPGA tools Xilinx, Circuits schematic design and verification
Understanding of VLSI, DSP, Wireless Communication Protocols, • Embedded systems and Internet of Things, Analog and Digital Circuits and Signal processing
Analog and Digital Mixed VLSI Design.
Knowledge in wafer level characterization - parametric analyzer, etc.,
Knowledge in CMOS technology libraries.
Fundamentals of Computer Architecture, Arithmetic Units Design, Bus protocols Architecture and Design, Memory Controllers Design and SoC Design.
Experience in prototyping the developed IP cores on FPGAs.
Journal and Conference publications of repute.
Expectations
Writing Verilog RTL coding for wireless protocols – PHY and MAC layers
FPGA/ASIC implementation of wireless protocols
Leading a team on SoC design and verification.
Project management tools including Version control and management tools
Excellent oral and written communication skills.
Knowledge and experience on technical publications and patent process.
Consolidated Pay Per Month
As per the industry standards based on qualification, experience, expertise, role etc.
