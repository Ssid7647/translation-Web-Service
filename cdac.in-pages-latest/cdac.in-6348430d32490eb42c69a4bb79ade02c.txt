E-Learning
Design & Development of e-Learning contents for e-Security Solution Developers
The Course consists of e-Learning contents on both introductory as well as advanced e-security concepts and applications aimed at providing advanced e-security knowledge on the mechanisms and models of e-security & its solutions.
The course is divided into 10-12 modules, beginning with the introductory concepts to more advanced implementations, whereby the learner can access the subsequent modules only after the successful completion of the earlier module (s).
The contents would be available in e-learning mode with audio explanation and simulations along with expert speaker's video lectures delivered by experts on selected topics.
The course will provide the learner
Knowledge on e-Security concepts
Understanding of the e-Security measures/solutions available and their usage
Application-specific knowledge of the subject along with the theoretical knowledge of basic concepts
Curriculum approved by eminent e-Security experts.
