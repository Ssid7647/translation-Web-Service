L band transmitter
Brief Description
L band transmitter is a L Band power amplifier unit with all desirable features in addition to monitoring and protection circuits.
Main uses and domain
This product is intended to be used by Defence and other professional domains
Features and Technical Specifications
Features:
Freq. range: 950MHz-1250MHz
Transmit power 50W
LDMOS transistor based design
Tunable receive front end with co-site filters
Better manufacturability due to distributed matching network
Transmit power : 50W
Harmonics :<50dBc
Power control :+/-1dB
DC power supply : 32V
Platform required (if any)
