Indread
July 01, 2015
Centre for Development of Advanced Computing, CDAC is going to launch four mobile applications today in Hyderabad to mark the Digital India Week (DIW).
In order to create receptive and transparent Prime Minister Narendra Modi is going launch most ambitious project ‘Digital Indi’ on Wednesday, July 1 in the national capital at 4:00 pm.
As a part this programme, Centre for Development of Advanced Computing is going to launch four mobile applications in Hyderabad to mark Digital India Week (DIW).
CDAC Hyderabad director E.Magesh is going to launch four mobile applications including ‘rice vocs mobile app’, RKMP e-learning mobile app, potential fishing zone advisory’s (PFZ) mobile app and joy of learning mobile app and announced the functionalities of all four apps.
Rice vocs mobile app: This mobile application is about to know different types of rice varieties and it can be used by agriculture extension officers or research scholars.
RKMP e-learning mobile app: As per CDAC Hyderabad is app is about to access Rice knowledge Management Portal’s (RKMP) e-learning course material.
Potential fishing zone advisory’s (PFZ): This mobile application can be used to get fishing zone advisories.
Joy of learning mobile app: This app is designed mainly for school children of three to 11 years of age, for accessing science experiment details.
