C-DAC Enquiry/Grievance Management System
C-DAC Enquiry/Grievance Management System:
For enquiries or clarifications and any grievances, C-DAC can be contacted via eMail, letter, fax or telephone.
The relevant contact details are as under:
Enquiries related to PACE program: pace@cdac.in
Enquiries related to GIST products: support.gist@cdac.in
Enquiries related to GIST products: sales.gist@cdac.in
Enquiries related to ACTS program: acts@cdac.in
Enquiries related to other issues: techsupport@cdac.in
