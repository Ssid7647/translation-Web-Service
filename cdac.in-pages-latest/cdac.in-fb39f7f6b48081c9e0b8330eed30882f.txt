Setting up of Centre of Excellence in IT at Vanuatu
Project is funded by MEA and CDAC is acting as Indian Implementing Agency & Vanuatu Institute of Technology in Port Vila as Vanuatu’s implementing agency.
DPA-II division, MEA & CDAC have signed an Agreement on Nov 16, 2016 for setting up of "India – Vanuatu Centre of Excellence in IT".
Project consists of:
Creation of IT infrastructure (IT hardware/ software, computer peripherals, networking equipment, classroom equipment, video conferencing and UPS etc.)
Course material & reference books for students undergoing training.
Supply, installation & training on CDAC’s LILA, e-learning software and learning management system with required digital content.
Deputation of 2 CDAC experts to Vanuatu for a period of 2 years each to assist in centre coordination & course delivery.
Training of 6 participants from Vanuatu for a period of 6 months at ACTS CDAC in India on advance IT courses.
CDAC’s certificate to the successful candidates passing out of CEIT in Vanuatu & ACTS CDAC in India.
Exchange of experts between India & Vanuatu for training, e-learning & project management.
