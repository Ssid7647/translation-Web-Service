Big Data Management & Comprehensive Analysis
Nominations are invited from Scientists & Technologists working in Government Sector for the Online Training Programme sponsored by Department of Science and Technology, Government of India under “NATIONAL PROGRAMME FOR TRAINING OF SCIENTISTS & TECHNOLOGISTS WORKING IN GOVERNMENT SECTOR”.
Detail of the Online Training programme: https://training.dst.gov.in
Training Brochure
Schedule for the training programme:
Starting Date:
29th November, 2021 – 03rd December, 2021
(1 Week/3 Sessions per Day)
Target Group:
The Scientists/Technologists holding scientific posts / working in the Scientific Department/Ministries of Govt. of India and State Governments, Autonomous Institutions, Public Sector Undertakings of Central (Group A only ) / State Governments, Research and Development Institutions/ Research Laboratories of Central / State Governments, Central / State Universities, State Science & Technology Councils.
Group-A officer serving in Govt. organization.
The upper age limit is 58 years as on July 1, 2021.
No. of years of service as Group-A scientist (5 Years & above).
Mode of training:
Online (3 sessions per day)
Submission Method:
Kindly visit (https://training.dst.gov.in), Department of Science & Technology, Training Cell online portal for submitting online nominations for the training programmes conducted under scheme "National Programme for Training of Scientists/Technologists working in Government Sector".
No. of Seats:
Age limit:
Not more than 58 years as on 1st July 2021
Programme Content:
See Training Brochure
Introduction:
C-DAC Mohali is conducting an Online Training Programme on Big Data Management & Comprehensive Analysis for the Scientists & Technologists working in Government sector from 29.11.2021 to 03.12.2021.
Course Objective:
To present approaches for Data analysis and overview of tools & techniques facilitating Data Analytics on huge datasets.
To apprise the participants about the Big Data Concepts & its processing, Data visualization, application of big data analytics technologies for different usage scenarios.
To provide the structured forum for peer to peer interaction and knowledge sharing among the scientific community for better networking and synergy.
Submission Method: Kindly visit (https://training.dst.gov.in), Department of Science & Technology, Training Cell online portal for submitting online nominations for the training programmes conducted under scheme "National Programme for Training of Scientists/Technologists working in Government Sector".
Necessary Information:This is online training programme and fee/sponsorship of the training programme will be met by the Department of Science and Technology, Govt. of India.
Maximum two nominations per organization shall be permitted.
*Note: The participants are requested to fill the nominations at the earliest because seats are limited.
The nominations received shall be scrutinized as per eligibility of Participant.
The selection call letter for Online Training Programme shall be sent through e-mail.
The Norms, rules & regulations of the DST shall be followed for this Online Training Programme.
