Multilingual Computing & Heritage Computing
The Human-Centred Design and Computing Group, C-DAC, Pune was responsible for establishing and managing the National Multimedia Resource Centre during 1998-2008.
The most notable deliverable of this activity were CD Titles on Multimedia Rendering of Dnyaneshwari and Life & Work of Srinivasa Ramanujan: The Mathematical Genius.
The group has nurtured technical skills and capabilities for design and development of interactive games, 3D virtual walkthroughs, web/mobile/kiosk based multimedia applications for learning and other emerging multimedia technologies.
