Five-Day Online Certificate Course on Cyber Security & Ethical Hacking
Date: 13 July to 17 July 2020
Time: 10.00 AM - 4.30 PM ***
Cyber Security & Cyber Forensics is one of the key thrust areas of R&D activity.
C-DAC is focusing on developing solutions / products to fit into the Cyber security Ecosystem, in such a way that it can provide the needed expertise to various agencies at different levels of security architecture like biometrics, endpoint security, perimeter security, SCADA Security, Honeynets, web application security, malware detection & prevention solutions, unified threat management, wireless / mobile security and so on.
It offers services such as Information Security Consulting, Auditing and w.r.t. globally recognized and accepted standards such as ISO27001, Network Posture Assessment Services including Penetration Testing, Vulnerability Assessment, Web Application Security Assessment, Information Security Awareness and Training.
About the Course:
Introduction:
Increased Internet penetration for applications such as e-Governance, e-Commerce, e-Banking, digital payments, e-Learning, smart cities, etc. has resulted in an exponential rise in sophisticated attacks on Information Technology (IT) infrastructure.
This has lead to Cyber Security as an important aspect to secure our computers and networks.
Both government & industry are becoming the victims of cyber criminals, including companies working in security technology.
Cyber Security is going to be a much more serious problem in the coming years.
Hence, it becomes more important & essential to be aware of the challenges and methods to meet the new age attacks.
Course objective:
This Course Will Provide An Overview on Cryptography And Information Security, PKI And Digital Signature, IPV6 Addressing, Ethical Hacking, Various Attacks & Countermeasures, Wireless & Wi-Fi Security, Viruses, Trojans, Spywares, Security Issues In Android, Overview of Cyber Forensics
Target Audience:
Officials of State/Central Govt departments, PSUs, Corporate, System / Network Administrators, Technical Officers, State & National Law agencies and Individuals
Pre-requisite:
Knowledge of Computing & Internet Usage 2) Desktop/Laptop with atleast 4 GB RAM 3) Broadband internet connectivity is preferred to connect to C-DAC VPN for Hands-on
Intake:
Participants will be limited to a max of Thirty (30) seats and on first cum-first serve basis only.
In case of more participants, then they would be accommodated in the next batch
Methodology:
The course will be for five (5) days, followed by one day evaluation & certification (both written & Lab).
Certification is not mandatory.
Fee:
Course fee is Rs 15,000/- + GST @ 18% per participant.
Payment shall be made through NEFT/ RTGS in favor of C-DAC Bengaluru.
Bank details shall be provided
How to Register:
Participants shall send confirmation email to aswath@cdac.in
Last date for Registration and receipt of Course Fees: 08.07.2020
Course Contents (Theory Sessions) **
Session 1.1: Cryptography and Information Security - Intro / Symmetric & Asymmetric Keys / Security Threat & Attacks from IT Act perspective
Session 1.3: PKI and Digital Signature - Digital Signature Certificate / Authority / Trust model / Types / Classes / Lifecycle
Session 2.1: IPV6 Addressing - IPv6 vs IPv4/ IPv6 Addressing / IPV6 Application / Services
Session 2.3: Introduction to Ethical Hacking - Ethical Hacker / Why Ethical Hacking / Terminology / Phases of Ethical Hacking / Information Gathering / Network Discovery / Various tools used for Scanning
Session 3.1: Brute Force Attack & Counter Measures - Brute Force Attack / Types of Password Attacks / Denial of Service (DoS) / Types of DoS Attacks / Symptoms for DoS Attack / MAC Address Spoofing
Session 3.3: Trojan, Backdoor & Virus and Android Hacking - Trojan, Backdoor & Virus / Tools Used / countermeasures / Malware Analysis & Types
Session 4.1: Wireless & Wi-Fi Security - Wireless Attacks and Threats / Wireless Protocols / Wireless Security Configuration and Network Isolation
Session 4.3: DNS Hacking and Countermeasures - DNS Concepts and fundamentals / DNS Zones and Domains / DNS Hierarchy / Various Attacks on DNS Infrastructure
Session 5.1: Network Defense & Counter Measures - Firewall / IDS / IPS / Unified Threat Management, Over of Cyber Forensics - Principles and Forensics Analysis
Session 5.3: MCQ & Hands-on Evaluation
