COPS-TARA Data Concentrator Unit (DCU)
Brief Description
COPS-TARA DCU (Data Concentrator Unit) is a communication device to transfer meters data to control center for real-time access.
DCU collects data from meters using MODBUS/DLMS standard protocol and sends to control center over OFC/Wired Internet/GSM/GPRS/3G/4G networks in real-time basis.
Product Features:
Used for implementation of SAMAST and Automatic Meter Reading solutions
Standard MODBUS/DLMS (Device Language Message Specification) protocol compliance
Meter data communication over OFC/Wired Internet/GSM/GPRS/3G/4G
Overcomes the Round Trip Time
Communication diagnosis through LED indications
Supports multiple meters
Economical solution to transfer meter data to control center
19" inch Rack mountable
Domain and Main usage:
Domain: Power sector / Power Utilities
Usage: For providing Automatic Meter Reading (AMR)/AMI/SAMAST Solutions
Platform required (if any): - NA
