Cyber Security and Cyber Forensics
Biometrics
Brief Description:
Classify fingerprint database in Arch, Left Loop, Right Loop and whorl classes.
Main uses and domain :
Law & Enforcement Agencies.
Features and Technical Specifications:
Fingerprint classification reduces the search space of a large database: Determine the class of the query fingerprint.
Then, only search templates within the same class as the query.
Platform required (if any):
Works on Windows, Linux and Android.
