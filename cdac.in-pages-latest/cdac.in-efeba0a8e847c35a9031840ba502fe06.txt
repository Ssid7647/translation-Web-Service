Name of Post
CAE - III (14 years and above experience)
Specialization/ Domain
C-DAC Adjunct Engineer - Solution Architect - Enterprise Application
No. of Requirements
Job Location
Delhi, Pune
Desired Educational Qualification and specialization
B. E. / B. Tech./ME/ M. Tech in Comp/IT/ Electronics/ Electronics & Telecommunication/communication/Electrical/ Electrical & Electronics
OR
MCA
OR
First Class PG Degree in Science or domain, in relevant discipline
Post Qualification relevant Experience
For - BE/ B. Tech / MCA/ ME/ M. Tech/ First Class PG Degree in Science or domain, in relevant discipline - 14 years and above
Max Age
57 years at the time of the submission of application
Desired Skill Sets
Experience in guiding mixed teams of business analysts, data scientist, UI-UX Leads and development teams in designing and implementing enterprise class big data applications with extensive use of AI, ML, and NLP etc.
Provide architectural blueprints and documentation of requirements using industry standard platforms
Hands on knowledge of databases, Microservices, full stack web-application development and their integration with platform applications like AD, LDAP, certificate services, email etc.
Specific competencies required in building applications using ELK stack, Neo4J graph databases, PostgreSQL, Apache Kafka, Java, Python, JavaScript.
Industry certifications on the specified platforms will be plus
Experience in design and integration of ETL tools including crawlers, web scrapers etc.
Expertise in Performance Tuning, Scalability, Design, Build, Test and Deployment
Experience in big data projects in the public sector is desirable
Proposed Job Profile
The selected Solution Architect will play a key role in the design of the complete application portfolio of a large turnkey project pertaining to the delivery of enterprise class big data applications with extensive use of AI, ML, and NLP etc. for a public sector organization over a period of 2-3 years.
Will report to the Senior Technical Program Manager (Applications) and be responsible for the mapping of business requirements to architecture over the lifecycle of the project with multiple releases.
Will have full ownership of the design covering functionality, performance, availability, security and privacy and take responsibility for integration with internal and external modules
Consolidated Pay Per Month
As per the industry standards based on qualification, experience, expertise, role etc.
