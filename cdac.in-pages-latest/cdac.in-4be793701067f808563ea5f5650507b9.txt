WORKSHOP
ON
HIGH PERFORMANCE COMPUTING
(WHPC 2015)
JULY 29-31, 2015
Jointly Organized by:
Department of Applied Mathematics Indian School of Mines (ISM), Dhanbad, Jharkhand (India)
Centre for Development Of Advance Computing (C-DAC), Pune, Maharashtra (India)
Venu : Golden Jubilee Lecture Theatre, ISM, Dhanbad.
About Workshop
Download Technical Programme »
Download Workshop Brochure »
Center for Development of Advanced Computing (C-DAC) Pune, and Department of Applied Mathematics, Indian School of Mines (ISM) Dhanbad, are jointly organizing three days technology workshop on broad overview of High Performance Computing (HPC) focusing on multi-core systems and GPUs.
(WHPC - 2015) ” which is scheduled from July 29-31, 2015.
This workshop will provide a broad overview of High Performance Computing (HPC) focussing on multi-core systems and GPUs.
The Supercomputing experts of CDAC will be involved in HPC lectures and Hands-on Session.
The workshop serves as a basic HPC training for running advanced application programs efficiently, on Clusters with accelerators.
Participants will also get exposer of HPC to pursue further studies in specific areas such as: computational fluid dynamics, modeling &simulation, seismic tomography, weather forecasting, aerodynamic research, probabilistic analysis, Brute force code breaking, 3D nuclear test simulations, Molecular Dynamics Simulation, N-body simulation, Galactic Dynamics etc.
