Spatial Sciences and Disaster Management
Indigenous Multilingual GPS-based Mobile Navigation Solution
Key Features
Displays .shp, .kml, satellite data & other intrinsic formats
Creates paths & saves GPS paths in vector formats
Replays tracks
Warns when a planned route is not followed or while approaching hazardous/restricted area
Facilitates Geo-tagging, an option to take photographs with automatic inscribing of geographic coordinates
Aids in rescue operation.
If an emergency button is pressed by the user, the rescuer can trace the location of the user.
Useful for BPO employees, parents, police, hospital, etc.
Service from mobile service providers not required except for the rescue option
Enabled Hindi, English & Malayalam currently
The system is operational in Windows Mobile having an inbuilt GPS
