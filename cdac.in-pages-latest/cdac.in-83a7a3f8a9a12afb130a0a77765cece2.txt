Two Day National Conference PARCOMPTECH India 2017
Two-Day National Conference, PARCOMPTECH India 2017
Date: 23-24 February 2017 @ NIAS Auditorium, IISc.
Bangalore
C-DAC Bangalore, is organizing a Two-Day National Conference, PARCOMPTECH India 2017, in technical collaboration with Bangalore Chapter of IEEE and CSI, during 23-24 February 2017, at NIAS, IISc.
Bangalore.
The conference features keynotes and tutorials on current standards in technology providing valuable insights by eminent Scientists and researchers working on thematic areas of the conference.
Authors of accepted papers shall present their genuine scientific research and inspire the next generation of scholars/scientists/researchers.
Target Audience:
PARCOMPTECH INDIA 2017 will be of interest to scientific & engineering community from R & D, academia and industry who want to :
get exposure to trends in Parallel Computing technologies
interact with technology experts
introduce & promote Parallel Computing in their organization/institution
develop Parallel Computing related skills.
