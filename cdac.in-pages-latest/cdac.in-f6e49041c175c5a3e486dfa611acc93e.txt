Office Contact Information
Students having enquiries can contact C-DAC's nearest ACTS as applicable similarly the GIST PACE Bureaus as applicable.
Their details are available under respective pages of this website.
Further, in order to facilitate clients with their enquiries, the C-DAC website has hosted a comprehensive 'Frequently Asked Questions' (FAQ) section, which addresses minor enquiries and complaints about GIST products.
There is also a feedback mechanism for selected GIST products to enable customers in providing feedback on their experience with our products.
All other enquiries may be directed to Webmaster.
The Webmaster will forward the complaint to the concerned group/member for necessary follow-up.
