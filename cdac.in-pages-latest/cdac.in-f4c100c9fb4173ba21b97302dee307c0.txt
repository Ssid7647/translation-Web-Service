COPS SCADA Lab Kit
Brief Description
C-DAC Open Process Solution (COPS) is an indigenously developed generic SCADA engine by Real-Time Systems and IoT Group.
The COPS architecture is scalable, hierarchical and distributed.
All the subsystems of COPS are compliant to the industry standards.
The different versions of COPS have been deployed successfully in Load Dispatch Centers of the Power Sector, steel plants across the country for energy management.
The current version of COPS is the result of more than 100 man-years of continuous effort and experiences gained over the years while delivering the SCADA solutions.
Now the COPS has also been packaged as SCADA Lab Kit for training and educational purposes.
COPS can be integrated with IEC 60870-5-101/104 compliant RTU and the developed RTU PUSPAC ( Portable Supervisory Unit and Programmable Automation Control).
Main uses and domain
For the establishment of SCADA Research Lab in Educational Institutions for conducting research, training and advanced studies in the area of SCADA.
domain: Process Industry
Features and Technical Specifications
IEC 60870-5-101/104 compliant RTU simulator
IEC 60870-5-101/104 compliant Master
User-friendly and rich GUI
Configurable reporting system and real-time trending
Tag Database configurator
Real-time and historical database management
Alarm/Event Handling
SCADA Human Machine interface
Platform required (if any): Severs
