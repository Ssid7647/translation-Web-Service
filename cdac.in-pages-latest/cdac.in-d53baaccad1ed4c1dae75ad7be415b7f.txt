C-DAC Adjunct Engineer - UI / UX Engineer
No. of Requirements
Job Location
Desired Educational Qualification and specialization
B E/B Tech/ ME / M Tech in Computer Science and Engineering / Information Science / Human Computer Interface / Graphics or similar
Experience
8 - 10 years Relevant experience as indicated in competence/expectations only will be considered
Max Age
57 years at the time of the submission of application
Competence (one or more from the list)
UI Full Stack Design and Development with hands-on experience.
Demonstrated experience User Interface design and Human Factors.
Experience in visualization and rendering technologies.
Experience in creating wireframes/mock-ups/prototypes.
Design and develop Augmented/Virtual/Mix Reality solutions.

Create/Architect UI solutions for various types of systems.
Ensuring cross-platform optimization for hybrid deployment including mobile platforms.
Seeing through a project from conception to finished product.

Strong organizational and project management skills.

Knowledge and experience on technical publications and patent process.
