Instructions
Use Form Number and Password that you have used while registering for CCAT exam for logging in to the Counselling & Seat Allocation System.
In case you forgot password, click on the Forgot Password link provided beside the Login button.
Password change link will be mailed to your email-id given during CCAT registration.
Before choice filling, check course eligibility given in pages 2 and 3 of the Admission Booklet.
Information about fee payment is provided in the Admission booklet.
Incase of any queries, mail to course-enquiry@cdac.in and actssupport@cdac.in
