Dr Hemant Darbari, Director General, C-DAC in discussion with Smt Sushma Swaraj, External Affairs Minister for Vishva Hindi Sammelan
"Dr Hemant Darbari, Director General, C-DAC in discussion with Smt Sushma Swaraj, External Affairs Minister, Smt Preeti Saran, Secretary (East), MEA at MEA, New Delhi for promoting and proliferating C-DAC language technologies during Vishva Hindi Sammelan (World Hindi Conference) scheduled to be held at Mauritius in August 2018.
The conference consists of several Hindi scholars, writers and laureates from different parts of the world to contribute the language.
Dr. Ajai Kumar, Associate Director, Shri Karimullah Shaik, Associate Director, C-DAC, Pune and Shri Abhishek Das, OSD to DG, C-DAC were present."
