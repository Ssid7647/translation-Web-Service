Land Resources
Landuse/Landcover Mapping
Study Area:  Bihar & Central Uttar Pradesh
Project Highlights
Level III mapping of Landuse/ Landcover from three cropping season satellite data using visual image interpretation techniques
Extensive ground validation
As input to National Natural Resources Census programme of Govt. of India
National Land Degradation Mapping using Multi-Temporal dataset
Study Area:  Bihar
Project Highlights
Mapping based on Land degradation (process, type & severity), Landform and Landuse
Physico-chemical analysis of degraded soils
As input to National Natural Resources Census programme of Govt. of India
Thematic Layers' Delineation from IRS P6 LISS-III data
Study Area:  Rajasthan, Gujarat, Maharashtra, Goa, Daman-Diu
Project Highlights
Thematic layers generated: Landuse/ Landcover, Geology (formation & structure) and Geomorphology
Extensive ground survey to validate the landuse/ landcover, geomorphic and geological maps
