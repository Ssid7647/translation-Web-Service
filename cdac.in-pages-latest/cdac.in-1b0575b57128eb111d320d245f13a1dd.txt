Intelligent Transportation Systems
Brief Description
TraMM is the Data acquisition system and user interface of C-DAC traffic signal controllers CUTE, WiTraC and UTCS
Main uses and domain
Remote monitoring and administration of road traffic signal controllers
Features and Technical Specifications
Provides interface between C-DAC traffic signal controllers and Adaptive Traffic Control System (CoSiCoSt).
Generates reports and views on the traffic signal controller operation.
Platform required (if any)
Windows
