Name of Post
C-DAC Adjunct Engineer - CAE (8 years and above)
Specialization/ Domain
C-DAC Adjunct Engineer – Project Manager (ECGC)
No. of Requirements
Job Location
Desired Educational Qualification and specialization
First Class Bachelor's degree or equivalent
Post Qualification relevant Experience.
Max Age
57 years at the time of the submission of application
Skill Set
Ability to manage, monitor overall project lifecycle with respect to QA objectives, milestones/deliverables, schedules, project resources, budget, tangible and intangible risk.
Good knowledge of project management methodologies, tools, techniques and KPIs.
Desirable: PMP certification, and PMI ACP, CSM, PSM etc.
Proposed Job Profile
Coordinating internal resources and stakeholders for the flawless execution of projects
Ensuring that project is delivered on-time, within scope and within budget
Measuring project progress and performance using appropriate systems, tools and techniques
Risk Management
Creating and maintaining comprehensive project documentation
Consolidated Pay Per Month
Salary will be decided based on qualification, experience, expertise, etc. Here is the salary structure:
Experience Band
8 – 10 years of experience
Up to Rs. 1,20,000/-
11 – 13 years of experience
Up to Rs. 1,51,000/-
14 – 16 years of experience
Up to Rs. 1,86,000/-
17 – 19 years of experience
Up to Rs. 2,20,000/-
20 or More years of experience
Up to Rs. 2,50,000/-
Note: There is a provision of HRA and Medical Reimbursement also.
