Inauguration of New Facility of C-DAC ACTS
Shri P P Chaudhary, Hon'ble Minister of State for Law & Justice and Electronics & IT, Govt of India inaugurated the new facility of the Advanced Computing Training School (ACTS) at C-DAC Innovation Park, Pune on November 19, 2016.
Also present on the occasion were Prof Rajat Moona, Director General, C-DAC, Dr Hemant Darbari, Executive Director, C-DAC, Pune, and other senior members of C-DAC and ACTS.
The new facility will incorporate both classrooms and state-of-the-art IT laboratories to cater to the different training programmes conducted by C-DAC ACTS.
Shri P P Chaudhary also released two new products, namely the Mobile IP App, a mobile based application to provide IPR services and e-Reception, a tablet based mobile reception desk as a part of the event.
