hyPACK-2013 Keynote talks & Invited talks
C-DAC experts in the parallel processing technologies areas like Multi-Core Processors with coprocessors and GPU accelerators , GPGPUs & GPU Computing, and HPC Cluster with coprocessors & accelerators and will deliver classroom lectures s in  Mode-1  &  Mode-2  programme.
Experts from Private Sectors IT Companies and C-DAC HPC experts deliver keynote talks on Intel Xeon-Phi Coprocessors and GPGPUs and demonstrate performance of Numerical Linear Algebra and Applicaiton Kernels.
Experts from Private Sectors IT Companies deliver keynote talks on Intel Xeon-Phi Coprocessors and NVIDIA & AMD GPGPUs as given in four days technical Programme and demonstrate Software tools from application point of view.
Experts from leading Academic Organizations may be invited to deliver invited talks on Multi-Core Processor systems with coprocessors & GPGPUs /GPU Computing and HPC GPU Cluster
Distinguished faculty from IISc, IIT, IIITs, NITs, University of Hyderabad and Indian Universities, may deliver keynote talks.
Professional Software experts from leading private sector IT companies such as  Intel, AMD, HP, NVIDIA, IBM, Cray  may deliver keynote talks & on Software tools on Multi-Core Processor systems with Intel Xeon Phi coprocessors and GPU accelerators and performance tuning aspects on GPGPUs.
Professional Software experts from various private sectors IT companies may be invited to deliver talks or demonstrate software on Multi-Core Processor Systems, Intel Xeon Phi Coprocessors, and GPGPUs, focusing on Compiler Accelerator Programming model for GPGPUs, Performance tuning using libraries on Multi-Core Processors.
Professional Software experts from leading private sector IT companies such as  IBM, Cray  may deliver keynote talks & on energy efficiency issues of HPC Cluster with coprocessors and accelerators
