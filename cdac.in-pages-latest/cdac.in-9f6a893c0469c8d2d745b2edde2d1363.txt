Shri Narendra Modi, Hon'ble Prime Minister of India launched Indigenous Automatic Fare Collection System based on One Nation One Card - National Common Mobility Card (NCMC) today at Ahmedabad.
The Automatic Fare Collection system - Swagat (Gate) and Sweekar (Software for control, fare collection and settlement with banks) has been jointly developed by C-DAC and BEL with support from DMRC, NPCI and SBI under guidance of MoHUA.
