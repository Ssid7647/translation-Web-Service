C-DAC, ACTS is conducting Post Graduate Degree awarding programmes in collaboration/affiliation with the well known Universities.
One of the approaches to tackle the problem of lacking job readiness in the Indian IT sector is partnerships between the industry and academia.
It is C-DAC Initiative in collaboration with universities to equip students with the understanding of “Industry-Academia Zero Gap Learning” and developing them as per current industry trends.
Course Highlights & Benefits
Zero Gap Between the Industry and Academia
With World Class Amenities (Digital Library/IEEE &ACM Springers Access)
Learning by Practical Orientation of Modules
First year training will be conducted at university campus, second year dissertation work will be at C-DAC Research Innovation and Incubator Lab
Learning by assessment approach
Lectures and Training by C-DAC/ICT Industry Expert
Hands-on training for each and every Module.
Dissertation work at C-DAC Innovation and Incubator Lab
Guidance will give by C-DAC / ICT Industrial expert at research labs
Interaction with various ICT industrial researchers at single platform of II Labs
Degree awarded by Collaborated University
Students will be assist for C-DAC Common campus Placement Programme

MTech - VLSI Design
CourseDetails:
The M.Tech.-VLSI at C-DAC Mohali is an intensive skill based and practice oriented program, which includes assignments, industry relevant projects, and dissertation work, are delivered through regular in house and expert guest faculty in the region with extended laboratory operations to provide stimulation and high end teaching learning experience.
C-DAC Mohali introduced the Master of Technology in VLSI Design at its campus in the year 2004 to cater to the demands of the field of digital electronics in India.
VLSI design is an intrinsic part of electronics today with digital design being implemented on FPGA.
As the complexity of digital circuits is increasing with time, thus we commit to generate quality engineers with skills to conquer the challenges in field of electronics.
The program incorporates regular assignments and high quality thesis work.
All laboratories are well equipped with adequate number of Industry standard Design Tools supported with FPGA, DSP and Microcontroller development boards.
VLSI labs are facilitated with Cadence EDA tools having 40 licenses for academic & Research purpose and 30 FPGA kits and 20 AVR kits.
The laboratory facilities are available for 24 hours and 7 days a week to provide a stimulating and high-end learning experience.
The program is conducted and monitored by highly qualified faculty with sole aim to produce the highest quality Industry ready manpower equipped to take up challenging assignments in Research and Industry in the areas of VLSI Design.
M.Tech- VLSI is duly approved by AICTE & affiliated to IKG-PTU Jalandhar.

M.Tech – Embedded Systems
The M.Tech.-Embedded Systems at C-DAC Mohali is an intensive skill based and practice oriented program, which includes assignments, industry relevant projects, and dissertation work, are delivered through regular in house and expert guest faculty in the region with extended laboratory operations to provide stimulation and high end teaching learning experience.
C-DAC, Mohali started M.Tech in Embedded System (ES) in the year 2011.
The program is well-designed and is conducted on a semester basis.
The objective is to build a strong foundation and develop the required skill set in students associated with the Electronics and Information Technology, Electronic Product Design and Embedded Systems.
The ES Labs include PCB Design Lab and Micro Controllers Lab, which are equipped with latest softwares like RTOS, RTLinux, OrCad, MATLAB, AVR Studios, Keil compiler, Micro C, Visual DSP++ 3.5, MP Lab compiler and in hardware ARM, PIC kits.
Students are motivated to take up research in their field of interest and full support is provided to design and develop a market ready product during the thesis work.
M.Tech- Embedded Systems is duly approved by AICTE & affiliated to IKG-PTU Jalandhar.

M.Tech – Electronic Product Design and Technology (EPDT)
The M.Tech.-EPDT at C-DAC Mohali is an intensive skill based and practice oriented program, which includes assignments, industry relevant projects, and dissertation work, are delivered through regular in house and expert guest faculty in the region with extended laboratory operations to provide stimulation and high end teaching learning experience.Besides imparting theoretical knowledge, a lot of stress is laid on hands on training and overall development of the individual's personality.Earlier, C-DAC had joined hands with PEC University erstwhile Punjab Engineering College, Chandigarh in the year 1994 to provide faculty to conduct theM.Tech program in Electronics Product Design & Technology (EPDT).
Later on C-DAC has started this course at its own campus at Mohali with affiliation from PTU, Jalandhar and approved by AICTE in 2011.
The programme is well-designed and is conducted on semester basis.
The objective is to build a strong foundation and develop the required skill set among the students in the field of Electronic Product Design & Technologies, Embedded Systems, Communications and Electronics and Information Technology.
The EPDT Labs include CAD/CAM Lab, PCB Design Lab and Micro Controllers Lab, which are equipped with latest softwares like AutoDesk Inventor, OrCad, MATLAB, AVR Studios, Keil compiler, Micro C, Visual DSP++ 3.5, MP Lab compiler and in hardware ARM, PIC kits.
M.Tech- EPDT is duly approved by AICTE & affiliated to IKG-PTU Jalandhar.

M.Tech – Information Technology (Part Time)
The M.Tech.-IT at C-DAC Mohali is an intensive skill based and practice oriented program, which includes assignments, industry relevant projects, and dissertation work, are delivered through regular in house and expert guest faculty in the region with extended laboratory operations to provide stimulation and high end teaching learning experience.
Off lately, CDAC observed the need for imparting higher education of professionals working in IT/ITES sector to update their skill sets.
To cater to the industry needs and the professionals, C-DAC Mohali introduced a part time course in Information Technology for professionals having at least 2 years of experience in different organizations viz. Central / State Government Autonomous Body / Public Limited Company / AICTE approved institute.
The program is well-designed and is conducted on semester basis for 3 years.
The objective is to build a strong foundation and develop the required skill set among the students in the field of Information Technology.
The IT Labs include Advanced Internet Technology, Advanced Software Engineering and Advanced Data Base Management with latest softwares.
M.Tech- Information Technology is duly approved by AICTE & affiliated to IKG-PTU Jalandhar.
