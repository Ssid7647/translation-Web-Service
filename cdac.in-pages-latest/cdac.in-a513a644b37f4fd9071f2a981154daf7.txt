Awards
The ongoing project "Centralized e-Auction System for North Eastern States (Sikkim, Assam, Arunachal Pradesh, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Manipur, Tripura and Nagaland)" of C-DAC Kolkata has been awarded the "SKOCH ORDER-OF-MERIT" award for qualifying amongst INDIA'S BEST – 2015 IN SMART GOVERNANCE at the 41st SKOCH Summit, 22nd-23rd September 2015, held at India Habitat Centre, New Delhi.
The project has been presented at the Summit by Shri Goutam Kumar Saha, Scientist-E, Head- Software Technology Group (STG) & Project Chief Investigator.
Project Team Members: Shri Goutam Kumar Saha (Sc-E, CI), Shri Debdulal Basak (Co-CI), Shri Manas Chakraborty, Shri Raja Gupta, Shri Sandeep Kumar, Shri Manish Saha, Shri Krishna Das, Shri Sandeep Sen, Shri Niladri Sekhar Saha, Shri Samaresh Das, Smt. Shimantika Shah, Ms Shampa De (STG, C-DAC Kolkata).
SKOCH Award Received by Shri Goutam Kumar Saha on behalf of the STG Project Team
