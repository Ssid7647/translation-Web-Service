Name of Post
CAE - II (11 years and above experience )
Specialization/ Domain
Adjunct Engineer - Natural Language Processing Expert / Computational Linguist
No. of Requirements
Job Location
Pune, Delhi
Desired Educational Qualification and specialization
PhD in Linguistics/Applied Linguistics with Computational Linguistics Training
Publication in NLP, Semantics/Lexicology, Discourse Domain will be an added advantage
OR
First Class PG Degree in Science or domain, in relevant discipline
Post Qualification relevant Experience
Ph. D /First Class PG Degree in Science or domain, in relevant discipline - 11 Years and above experience
Max Age
57 years at the time of the submission of application
Desired Skill Sets
Proficiency in Syntax, Semantics, Discourse and command over English Grammatical structures
Proficient in using computers including Microsoft office ( word/excel/PowerPoint)
Proficiency in Hindi and other Indian/foreign languages
Knowledge of using NLP tools like Part of speech tagger etc.
Well versed with conventions of Computational Linguistics like Penn Tagsets, NER tags etc.
Proposed Job Profile
Should be able to analyze Text to extract formal Syntactic/Semantic labels
Can test tagged/annotated text for relevant linguistic/grammatical categories like PoS/NER/Co-reference tags
Should be prepared to form rule-sets to build Natural Language Processing Software
Grammar Rules (English, Hindi or any other language) and Lexicon Creation
Proficient in creating documents without grammatical errors in Official/Reporting Styles of English
Comfortable to work in a team and ready to learn in Computational platform (NLP & AI)
Consolidated Pay Per Month
As per the industry standards based on qualification, experience, expertise, role etc.
